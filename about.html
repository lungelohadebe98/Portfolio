About me

Front-end developer from Johannesburg, South Africa

My Name is Lungelo Hadebe, I am from a small town called Memel. I have always showed interest in Technology but due to the lack of resources from our town, I could not get more knowledge or try to persue a career in Tech so instead when I got to university I tried to study Bachelor of commerce but could not obtain my degree on my final year due to financial difficulties, nonetheles my love for sofware development has always been there.

My passion for computers only intensified. Despite the lack of resources and opportunities in my town, I devoured every piece of information I could find online, teaching myself programming languages and delving into the basics of software development.

I then embarked on a journey to turn my dreams into reality. I spent countless nights honing my skills, working on personal projects, and participating in online coding communities. The more I learn, the more I yearn to make a mark in the tech world.

I hope as time passes, my skills will blossom, and my confidence soares. Become an integral part of the development team, contributing innovative solutions and bringing fresh perspectives to the table.
